# 1) Present Continuous

we use it to express an action that is currently happening


# 2) Present Simple 

we use it:

1. to talk about things in general
2. to talk about things that happen repeatedly
3. to talk about something true in general

# 3) Past Simple 

we use it to express an action that happened in the past and ended

# 4) Past Continuous

we use it to talk about what happened during the period of a past action

# 5) Present Perfect

we use it to talk about an action that happened in the past and the result of it still exist

# 6) Present Perfect Continuous

we use it for an activity that recently stopped or just stopped
